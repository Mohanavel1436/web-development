TAMIL NUDU

The architectural style of Tamil Nadu temples dates back to the Chola, Pandya, and Pallava dynasties, spanning over centuries. The evolution of these temples reflects the artistic and religious advancements of the time. Some of the most famous temples, like the Brihadeeswarar Temple in Thanjavur (built by the Cholas) and the Meenakshi Amman Temple in Madurai (constructed during the Pandya dynasty), showcase this rich heritage.
