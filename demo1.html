ANNA UNIVERSIITY

Anna University is a prestigious technical university located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1978, it offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, technology, and applied sciences. Known for its high academic standards and strong emphasis on research, the university is affiliated with hundreds of engineering colleges across Tamil Nadu. With a commitment to innovation and excellence in education, Anna University continues to play a significant role in shaping the future of engineering and technology professionals in India.

VIT VELLOR

VIT Vellore, established in 1984 by Dr. G. Viswanathan, is a premier private university located in Vellore, Tamil Nadu. It was conferred the status of a deemed university in 2001. Spanning over 372 acres, the campus is renowned for its state-of-the-art infrastructure and vibrant academic environment

IIT MADRAS

Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT Madras) is a premier public technical and research university located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1959 with technical and financial assistance from the Government of the former West Germany, it was the third IIT to be set up by the Government of India. Recognized as an Institute of National Importance, IIT Madras operates autonomously and is one of the eight public Institutes of Eminence in India.
